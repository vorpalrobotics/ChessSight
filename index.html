Queen Attack

#1
0:00 Misses: 0

A black king and one random black piece (rook, bishop, or knight) are placed on the board with a white queen that doesn't yet attack either. Click every square where the queen can move to fork (attacks both pieces), skewer (checks king, exposing the piece behind it), or pin the piece to the king. The square must be reachable by the queen, not adjacent to the king, and not immediately recapturable by the black piece. Click COMPLETE when you believe you have found all such squares — any you missed will flash.

Knight Route

#1
0:00 Misses: 0

Route the white knight to the target square (◎) in as few moves as possible. White pawns block the knight's path. Black pawns are also impassable, and the knight cannot move into any square a black pawn attacks (the two diagonals in front of it). Click any reachable square — valid moves turn green with the step number, yellow once you exceed the optimal count. Re-tap your current square to undo the last move. Click SHOW to reveal an optimal solution. You score 1 miss per invalid click plus 1 miss per move beyond the shortest possible route. A ⭐ marks a perfect run.
