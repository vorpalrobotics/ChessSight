Count Threats

#1
W
B
0:00 Misses: 0

Count moves that create a threat. A threat is a move where the piece lands safely (not immediately recapturable by a cheaper enemy piece) on a square from which it attacks a loose (undefended) enemy piece, attacks an enemy piece of strictly greater value (P=1, N/B=3, R=5, Q=9), or sets up checkmate in 1. Kings are excluded from the first two categories — those are checks. Pawn promotions to the same square count as one move.

Queen Attack

0:00 Misses: 0

Click every square where the white queen can move to create a fork (attacks both king and rook), skewer (queen checks king, exposing rook behind it), or diagonal pin (rook pinned to king along a diagonal). The square must be reachable by the queen, not adjacent to the king, and not attackable by the rook. Click COMPLETE when you believe you have found all such squares — any you missed will flash.
